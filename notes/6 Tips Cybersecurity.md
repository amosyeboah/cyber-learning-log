# Answers
Cybersecurity is the practice of ensuring confidentiality, Integrity, Availablity of Information, there by protecting
computer systems, networks, Data and People from digital attackers or hackers.

Skillsets:
1. Understanding of Networks
2. SIEM (splunk, chronicle)
3. Linux Operations
4. Programming Language ( Python, SQL, Bash scripting)
5. Security frameworks, principles and standard

Common Threats
1. Malware(Malious software) eg; virus, worms, ransomware and spyware
2. Social Enginnering attack (phising, vishing)
3. Dos/DDos - Denial(Distributed) of Service Attack
4. Mitm - Man in the Middle Attack
5. SQL Injection

Tools for SOC Role
1. Linux
2. SIEM tools (splunk)
3. Wireshark
4. tcpdump
5. Nmap

Security Principles
1. CIA triad
2. Defense In Depth
3. Principle of least Priveledge
4. Sepration of Duties
5. Non Repudiation
6. Risk Management
